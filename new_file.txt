Bangladesh is a deltaic nation in South Asia.
The country is home to one of the world's longest beaches, Cox's Bazar, which is 75 miles long.
Bangladesh is the home of largest university of ancient south asia and this is Shompur Mohabihara.
This is the home of largest mangrove forest which is Sundarbans.
Bangladesh is an overpopulated country.
This country's language is Bengali.
Bangladesh is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and unique geographical features.
Bangladesh's climate is Tropical.
This country has 8 divisions: Barishal, Khulna, Rajshahi, Chittagang, Sylhet, Rangpur, Mymensingh and Dhaka.
Most of the people in this country are Muslims.
